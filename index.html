Macro

Macro photography is a type of photography that involves taking close-up images of small subjects. The goal of macro photography is to capture the intricate details and textures of the subject, making it appear larger than life.

Portraits

Portraiture is the art of creating a photographic representation of a person or group of people. The main focus of portraiture is to capture the likeness, personality, and mood of the subject, often through the use of lighting, composition, and post-processing techniques. Portrait photography can be used for various purposes, such as personal keepsakes, business headshots, and fine art.

Wildlife

Wildlife photography is the art of capturing images of animals and other wildlife in their natural habitats. The goal of wildlife photography is to showcase the beauty and diversity of the natural world and to raise awareness of conservation issues.

Nature

Nature photography is the art of capturing images of the natural world, including landscapes, plants, and wildlife. The goal of nature photography is to showcase the beauty and diversity of the natural world, and to inspire a sense of wonder and appreciation for the natural environment. In other words, it is the parent of wildlife photography

Street and architecture

Street and architecture photography is the art of capturing images of the built environment, including streetscapes, buildings, and other architectural subjects. The goal of street and architecture photography is to capture the unique character and beauty of urban environments, and to showcase the relationship between people and the built environment.

Astro

Astro photography is the art of taking photographs of celestial objects and the night sky, including stars, planets, galaxies, and nebulae. The goal of astro photography is to capture stunning images of the night sky, and to showcase the beauty and wonder of the universe.

Event

Event photography is the art of capturing images of people and activities at events such as weddings, parties, conferences, and performances. The goal of event photography is to document the key moments and memories of the event, while also capturing the overall atmosphere and energy of the occasion.
